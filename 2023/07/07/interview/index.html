人大高瓴夏令营英文面试准备

TASK1: The most proud thing / the most satisfying project

I recently completed a project on “Image-to-Poem Generation”, which is a multimodal task of image and text. I think it is very interesting. This is not an easy problem to solve, because there are few papers that specifically discuss this task, and the paired image-poem dataset is also very scarce. So I adopted a two-stage approach: first, I retrieved some keywords related to the image, and then I generated a poem based on the keywords. For the first stage, first, I collected more than 6,000 keywords of ancient Chinese poems to form a dataset; second, I encoded these keywords into vectors using the text encoder of the pre-trained CLIP model; finally, for any landscape photo, I can encode it into an image vector using the image encoder of the CLIP model, and calculate the similarity with these keyword vectors, and select the top-k keywords with the highest similarity. For the second stage, we pre-trained a GPT2 model to generate poems from keywords. However, this project is far from over, and I will optimize it in the future with rich methods.

TASK2: Your favorite book / Movie

My favorite book and movie series is Harry Potter, which is a fantasy story about the magical world, written by the British author J.K. Rowling. There are seven books and eight movies in the series. I like Harry Potter because it is very imaginative and captivating, and it also teaches me some important lessons about friendship, courage, loyalty, and love. My favorite book and movie is the third one, Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban, because it has a lot of twists and turns, and it also introduces one of my favorite characters, Sirius Black, who is Harry’s godfather and a loyal friend. I think Harry Potter is a wonderful and inspiring work of art, and it has a special place in my heart.

TASK3: failure or frustration

One of my biggest setbacks was that I failed to win any awards in the International Mathematical Contest in Modeling for two consecutive years. It was the most popular contest in our school and attracted a lot of attention from my peers. Many students were able to achieve excellent results in their freshman or sophomore year, so I thought I should be able to do well too. However, I only got “Successful Participant” in both attempts. But I never gave up and learned from the outstanding papers and methods of previous years, and finally got Finalist Winner this year.

TASK4: About hometown

Ningbo is an international city in the eastern part of Zhejiang Province. Many people consider there “Rich in culture, and an important trading port.” There is a huge seaport here, called Zhoushan Port, which is one of the most important ports in China. There is a library with a long history, called Tianyi Pavilion, which preserves many historical relics. When talking about the delicacies in Ningbo, I can only tell you that Ningbo glutinous rice balls are quite famous, and the native chicken and pig on Siming Mountain are delicious.

TASK5: self introduction

尊敬的老师们：

你们好！我叫冯科华，来自西安电子科技大学人工智能学院图灵班。很荣幸来到高瓴人工智能学院，和各位优秀的老师面对面交流！下面我简要介绍一下我的个人情况。

本科就读期间，我在基础课和专业课的学习上一直严格要求自己，为未来的研究生旅程打下坚实的基础，曾获得校二等奖学金等荣誉。在前五个学期中，我的总评平均成绩为92.00 / 100，专业排名为3 / 35，绩点GPA 为3.9 / 4.0。同时，我在本科期间一直坚持英语学习，在CET-4中获得564分，在CET-6中获得559分。

我一直认为参加竞赛能够锻炼和提高我解决问题的能力。我参加过很多数学类相关竞赛，都取得了不错的成绩。其中，我获得了国际大学生数学建模竞赛的特等奖提名(Finalist)，获得了全国大学生数学建模竞赛省级一等奖、全国大学生数学竞赛省级一等奖等。在参加竞赛的过程中虽然有点挫折，但好在最终都被我逐个击破。

科研和项目是巩固自己所学的不错途径。我于2023年3月独立完成图像-文本多模态的项目，探索了图像-古诗生成器构建。由于图像-古诗对数据集的匮乏，初代模型采用两步的方法进行：从古诗关键词数据集中检索和图像匹配(CLIP)的top-k
个关键词，再通过关键词生成古诗(Decoder)。为了完成第一步，我引入了一个预训练的CLIP模型，通过文本编码和图像编码的相似度选择合适的关键词；为了完成第二步，我预训练了一个GPT2模型，使decoder可以根据输入的关键词生成古诗。

除上述项目外，我有一个省级大创《基于深度神经网络干扰鉴别方法研究》，使用Bi-LSTM完成航迹干扰鉴别，现已结题。我还做过很多NLP和CV方向的任务，例如基于Attention机制和Bi-LSTM的文本匹配、基于LSTM-CRF的序列标注(命名实体识别)、基于SRGAN的图像超分、基于Siamese网络的人脸配对等。我在众多的实践中熟练了Pytorch框架的使用，熟悉各种深度学习模型的理论。我对深度学习的热情逐渐高涨，想要深造研究的想法也更加成熟。

最后，我认为我是一个乐观有趣的人，有较多的爱好，尤其喜欢篮球和音乐。我也喜欢在空闲的时候写写书法，来提高自己的专注力。我相信我能给学院注入活力，做出贡献，谢谢！
